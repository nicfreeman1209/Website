Food & Drink

A light lunch and tea/coffee will be provided to all registered participants, and there will be a Conference Dinner on the Wednesday evening. For all other evening meals, see the page on Eating Out.

Hotels & Travel

Participants are asked to arrange their own accommodation. Several suggestions can be found on the Travel & Hotels page, along with travel advice and directions to the location of the conference.

Contributed talks, Poster session & Support for Ph.D. students

We have support available for travel and accommodation for twelve U.K. based research students, of up to £250 per person. If you would like to be considered for this, please contact easter_prob_2018@sheffield.ac.uk before March 9th.

We have a number of slots available for contributed talks. There will also be a poster session, at which early career researchers are invited to present their own work. If you would like to give either, please send a title and/or abstract to easter_prob_2018@sheffield.ac.uk. We will reimburse the cost of registration for all those who present.
