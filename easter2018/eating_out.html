Eating out in Sheffield

There are many nice places to eat within a short walk of the Hicks building. A good selection of them can be found on a 5 minute walk down Glossop Rd/West Str.

Some close-by suggestions for evening meals out:

  1. Las Iguanas (South/Latin American).
  2. Jin Ding (Chinese, Cantonese/Sichuan).
  3. Efes Restuarant (Turkish).
  4. Akbar's (Indian).
  5. Piccolo's (Italian).
  6. The University Arms (English, pub).
  7. The Doctor's Orders (English, pub).
  8. Pizza Express (Pizza).

Further away, up to 20 minutes walk, but highly recommended:

  1. Oisoi (Asian, fusion cooking).
  2. The Milestone (English, gastropub).
  3. Stew & Oyster (English, stews and oysters).
  4. VeroGusto (Italian).
  5. Edo Sushi (Sushi, takeaway only).
  6. The Blue Moon Cafe (Vegetarian/Vegan).

You can find more places on the trip advisor page for eating out in Sheffield.
