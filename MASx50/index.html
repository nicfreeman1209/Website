MAS350/451/6352 - Probability with Measure



This is the course web page for MAS350, MAS451 and MAS6352, known collectively as MASx50. Course notes, exercises and solutions can be found below.
To contact the lecturer, email n.p.freeman@sheffield.ac.uk.

Lectures are at 2pm on Thursdays and 12pm on Fridays, both in LT2 of the Hicks Building.

Exercises and solutions can be found inside the lecture notes (below).

Three times during each semester, assignments will be set in lectures, to be handed in, marked and also returned in lectures.

Office hours are Fridays 3-4 (before Easter) and at various times after Easter, in room 20 on floor I of the Hicks building. You may simply turn up, but priority will be given to those who book a time.

Assessment is by an exam, in the summer, at the end of the whole course.

Lectures will use the blackboard, and occasionally slides.
Students should bring their copy of the typed notes to lectures, for reference and annotation.
