Research Interests

My research interests are in probability, stochastic processes and population genetics. In particular:

  • Branching processes, interacting particle systems and random geometric models
  • Population genetics; particle based models of evolution in continuous space
  • Self-organized criticality and forest fire models
  • Stochastic analysis
Publications, pre-prints, code, talks, etc.

Teaching

I currently teach:

  • MAS223: Statistical Modelling and Inference
Selected material from older courses:
  • Martingale Theory with Applications. The course introduces conditional expectation, martingales and their applications to stochastic processes. Lectures notes, Problem Sheets
  • Itō Calculus and Complex Brownian motion. Itō calculus for semimartingales, planar Brownian motion, Picard's Little Theorem. Lectures notes, Problem sheets
