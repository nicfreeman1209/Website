Research Interests

My research interests are in probability, stochastic processes and population genetics. In particular:

  • Branching and coalescing processes, interacting particle systems and random geometric models
  • Population genetics; particle based models of evolution
  • Self-organized criticality
  • Preferential attachment and urn models
  • Stochastic analysis
