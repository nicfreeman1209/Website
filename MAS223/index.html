This is the course web page for the first semester of MAS223. Course notes (excluding some of the worked examples from lectures), exercises and solutions will be posted here.
To contact the lecturer, email n.p.freeman@sheffield.ac.uk.

Lectures will on Mondays at 12 noon (LT1, Dainton Building) and on Mondays at 3pm (LT1, Hicks Building). There will be no lectures in week 7, which is SoMaS reading week.

Exercises will be suggested on Mondays, in lectures, in weeks 2,4,6,9 and 11.

Tutorials are 12-1 on Tuesdays or 1-2 on Thursdays, depending on which group you are in, during weeks 2,4,6,9 and 11. You can check which group you are in here, and see the full timetable here.

Office hours are from 4-5pm, on both Tuesdays and Thursdays, G18 in the Hicks building, every week except week 7. Occasionally I may be away and hold them at a different time; this will be announced in lectures.

Assignments will be set via email (& below), on Thursdays by 6pm, in weeks (weeks 2,4,6,9 and 11). Each will be a subset of the exercises suggested that week.

Assignments will be due in by 4pm, on Tuesday, in the week after it was set (weeks 3,5,8,10 and 12). They should be handed directly to me in lectures, or to the dropbox at F10 stapled together with the right cover sheet for your tutorial group.

Solutions will be made available (below) by 5pm, on Tuesdays after work is handed in (weeks 3,5,8,10 and 12).

Assessment is by an exam at the end of the whole course, and a practical project in the second semester of the course. The reference sheet of distributions below will be made available in the exam.

Lectures will use slides. The content of the slides is also available in the form of typed lecture notes.
Lectures will also include worked examples on the boards - students should take lecture notes by hand for these.
