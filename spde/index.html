Food & Drink

A light lunch and tea/coffee will be provided on each day of the conference, to all registered participants. For evening meals, see the page on Eating Out.

Hotels & Travel

Participants are asked to arrange their own accommodation. Some suggestions for hotels can be found on the Travel & Hotels page, along with travel advice and directions to the location of the conference.

Support for Ph.D. students

We have partial funding available to help with travel and accommodation for 10 U.K. based Ph.D. students, of £200 per person. If you would like to be considered for this, please let us know by email at spde_2017@sheffield.ac.uk (before or after registering!).
